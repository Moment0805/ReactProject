Welcome to the Guessing Game!

How to play

  • The game starts with a hidden word, represented by a scoreboard with empty boxes.The player has a limited number of attempts to guess the word.
  • If the guessed letter is present in the hidden word, the scoreboard updates with the letter in the appropriate positions with green background. If the guessed letter is not present in the hidden word, a part of the hangman figure (represented by a spiral) is drawn, and the player loses an attempt.
  • The game continues until the player guesses the word correctly or uses up all their attempts.
